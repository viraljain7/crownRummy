Privacy Policy

Crown Rummy (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Crown Rummy website and mobile application (together “Platform”), which enables users in select states of India (“Users”, or “you” and its grammatical variations) to access and participate in the online game of Rummy in various formats and variations.

Accordingly, you are advised to read this Privacy Policy carefully before accessing the Platform.

All words used but not defined herein shall have the meaning ascribed to them in the Terms of Service.

1. Types of information we collect

1.1. We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide: When you register on the Platform, you will be required to provide us with your name, location, email address, mobile number, display picture, and other relevant profile information. Additionally, you may be required to provide your bank account details when you make use of the add cash or cash withdrawal options, and your Permanent Account Number (PAN) when you use the cash withdrawal option. In some cases, such as referral programs, you may provide us with the personal information of another person.
  • Transaction details initiated by you: If you initiate a cash transaction in relation to your Crown Rummy Account, we receive and store details pertaining to such transaction, including the payment method used, the amount added, and the date and time of the transaction.
  • Interaction information: When you interact with our customer support over any medium, such interactions may be recorded and stored.
  • Technical information about you: We automatically collect certain information from you when you use the Platform, including your IP address, location, unique device identifiers, browser type, language, the URL of the website which you may have used to reach our Platform, and cookies.
  • Usage information: When you play Games on our Platform, we collect details related to your use and interaction with our Platform and other Users, such as the date and time of access, location, money spent, duration of play, and game formats.
  • Messages: When you use our Platform to communicate, we receive and store the messages you send or receive and any content you upload or share.
  • Cookies: We use cookies (small text files) to store certain information each time you visit. These cookies help us recognize you and serve advertising banners. You can manage cookies in your browser settings, but disabling them may affect your experience.
  • Web beacons: We use web beacons (pixel tags) in combination with cookies to understand how you interact with our Platform. Disabling cookies may render web beacons ineffective.

1.2. Mandatory Information:

Some information required upon registration is mandatory. If you do not provide it, you may not be able to access some features of the Platform.

  • Enable your access and use of the Platform.

  • Verify and identify Users and their accounts.

  • Personalise the Platform, including ads served to you.
  • Provide customer support and enforce our Platform Policies.

2.2. Referrals and Promotions:

Where you use our referral program, we use your details to communicate with the person referred. We may also use your personal information for testimonials and other promotions with your consent.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services. Thereafter, we may retain it to comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements, subject to limitations under applicable law.
