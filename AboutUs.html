Our Story

Founded in 2015, Crown Rummy has grown from a passionate startup to India's premier online rummy destination. What began as a vision to revolutionize digital card gaming has evolved into a platform serving millions of players across India.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation in game technology, unwavering commitment to fair play, and dedication to responsible gaming practices. Today, we're proud to be recognized as an industry leader, setting standards for security, customer service, and player experience.
